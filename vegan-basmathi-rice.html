Vegan Basmati Rice Recipe

Fluffy, fragrant, and simple to make!

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

Step 1: Rinse the basmati rice in cold water until the water runs clear.

Step 2: Heat oil in a large pan, add the spices, and fry for 1 minute.

Step 3: Add the rinsed rice, water, and salt. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a simmer for 15 minutes.

Step 4: Remove from heat and let the rice sit covered for 5 minutes before serving.
