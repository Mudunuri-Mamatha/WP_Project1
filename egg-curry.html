Egg Curry Recipe

Spiced egg curry in a rich, flavorful sauce

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they crackle, add chopped onions and saut√© until golden.

Step 2: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute. Then add the tomato puree and spices, cooking until oil separates.

Step 3: Cut the hard-boiled eggs in half and gently add them to the curry. Cook for a few minutes.

Step 4: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or roti.
