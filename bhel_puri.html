Bhel Puri Recipe

Flavorful and crunchy Indian street food made with puffed rice, chutneys, and veggies.

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 cups puffed rice (kurmura or murmura)
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup boiled green peas
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind chutney
  • 1 tablespoon green chutney
  • Sev for garnish
  • Lemon juice to taste

Steps

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine the puffed rice, roasted peanuts, boiled green peas, chopped onions, tomatoes, and cilantro. Mix well to combine the ingredients evenly.

Step 2: Add cumin powder, chaat masala, tamarind chutney, and green chutney to the mixture. Stir gently until the puffed rice is coated with the spices and chutneys.

Step 3: Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the mixture and toss to distribute the flavors. Adjust seasoning and chutneys according to taste.

Step 4: Garnish the bhel puri with a generous handful of sev for extra crunch. Serve immediately, as bhel puri is best enjoyed fresh and crispy.
