Gulab Jamun Recipe

Classic Indian dessert, perfect for any celebration!

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 teaspoon rose water

Steps

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, combine the milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda. Add melted butter and mix well. Gradually add milk, a little at a time, and knead the mixture into a soft dough.

Step 2: Let the dough rest for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare the sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water in a pan over medium heat. Once the sugar dissolves, add cardamom powder and rose water. Let the syrup simmer for 5 minutes, then remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3: Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls, ensuring they are smooth and crack-free.

Step 4: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Gently slide the dough balls into the hot oil and fry them until they are golden brown. Make sure to fry them on low to medium heat for even cooking.

Step 5: Once fried, remove the gulab jamuns from the oil and immediately place them in the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak in the syrup for at least 2 hours before serving.

Step 6: Serve the gulab jamuns warm or at room temperature, garnished with chopped nuts if desired. Enjoy!
