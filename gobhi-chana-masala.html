Gobi Channa Masala Recipe

Cauliflower and chickpeas cooked in a rich and aromatic sauce

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 small cauliflower (cut into florets)
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas (or canned)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Oil for cooking
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Steps

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, saut√© onions until golden brown.

Step 2: Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute. Add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.

Step 3: Add the spices, salt, and cauliflower florets. Stir well and cook for 5-7 minutes.

Step 4: Add chickpeas and simmer for another 10 minutes until the cauliflower is tender.

Step 5: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan.
