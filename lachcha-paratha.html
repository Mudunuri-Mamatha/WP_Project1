Lachcha Paratha Recipe

Flaky, Layered Indian Bread

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 50 minutes

Servings: 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • Water, as needed for dough
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Butter for brushing

Steps

Step 1: Mix flour, salt, and water to form a soft dough. Rest for 30 minutes.

Step 2: Divide the dough into balls, roll each into a thin disc, and brush with oil/ghee.

Step 3: Fold, roll again, and cook on a tawa until golden on both sides. Brush with butter and serve.
