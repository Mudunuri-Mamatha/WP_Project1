Mutter Paneer Recipe

Delicious peas and paneer curry in a rich tomato-based sauce

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 4 to 6 servings

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 medium onions (chopped)
  • 3 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Oil for frying
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Steps

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan and fry the paneer cubes until golden. Set them aside.

Step 2: In the same pan, add cumin seeds. Once they crackle, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.

Step 3: Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute. Then, add the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.

Step 4: Add coriander powder, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes.

Step 5: Add peas and a little water. Cover and cook until the peas are tender.

Step 6: Stir in the fried paneer and garam masala. Simmer for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
