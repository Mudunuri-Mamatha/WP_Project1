Kulfi Recipe

Rich and creamy Indian ice cream dessert!

Preparation Ingredients Steps Nutrition

Preparation Time & Servings

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Freezing Time: 4 hours

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup condensed milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped pistachios
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped almonds
  • 1 tablespoon saffron strands (optional)

Steps

Step 1: In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring the whole milk to a boil. Reduce the heat to low and simmer the milk, stirring frequently to prevent burning, for 45 minutes or until the milk reduces to half its original volume.

Step 2: Add the sugar, condensed milk, cardamom powder, saffron strands (if using), and half of the chopped nuts to the milk. Stir well and cook for another 5 minutes until the mixture thickens slightly.

Step 3: Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down to room temperature. Once cooled, pour the mixture into kulfi molds or small cups, cover with foil, and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.

Step 4: To serve, remove the kulfi from the mold by dipping it in warm water for a few seconds. Garnish with the remaining chopped nuts and enjoy this creamy, delicious dessert!
